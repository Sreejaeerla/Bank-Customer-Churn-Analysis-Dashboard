# Bank Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

The Bank Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze customer attrition patterns and identify key factors influencing customer churn. The dashboard provides actionable insights into customer demographics, account characteristics, and behavioral trends to support customer retention strategies and data-driven decision-making.

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer churn trends and patterns
* Identify factors contributing to customer attrition
* Monitor key customer and account metrics
* Understand customer demographics and behavior
* Support retention and customer engagement strategies

## 🔑 Key Metrics

* Total Customers
* Churned Customers
* Active Customers
* Churn Rate (%)
* Customer Retention Rate (%)
* Average Customer Age
* Average Account Balance

## 📈 Dashboard Insights

### Customer Demographics Analysis

* Churn by Age Group
* Churn by Gender
* Churn by Geography

### Customer Behavior Analysis

* Churn by Credit Score
* Churn by Tenure
* Churn by Number of Products
* Churn by Active Membership Status

### Financial Analysis

* Churn by Account Balance
* Churn by Estimated Salary

### Business Performance Analysis

* Customer Retention Trends
* High-Risk Customer Segments
* Customer Segmentation Insights

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

* Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX
* Data Modeling
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Interactive Visualizations
* KPI Development

## 📌 Key Findings

* Customer churn varied significantly across different age groups and geographical regions.
* Inactive members showed a higher likelihood of churn compared to active customers.
* Customers with fewer products demonstrated higher churn rates.
* Customer behavior and account activity were strong indicators of retention risk.
